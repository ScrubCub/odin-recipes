Chicken Afritada

Description

Chicken afritada is a classic Filipino stew with a tomato sauce base and potatoes and carrots. This dish typically populates households during festivals due to its ease of cooking while still being delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken cutlets
  • 1 piece onion, diced
  • 1 bulb garlic, diced
  • 3 potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 3 carrots, peeled and cubed
  • 1 can peas, drained
  • 2 red bell pepper, sliced into strips
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1 pack tomato sauce
  • salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • (optional) two packs soda crackers, crushed

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pot
  2. Saute garlic and onion until slightly browned
  3. Add chicken cutlets until lightly browned
  4. Pour tomato sauce, add bell pepper, and add three cups water. Cover and let simmer for 40 minutes
  5. Add potatoes, carrots, and peas. Let simmer again until potatoes are soft
  6. Add fish sauce and optionally, crushed soda crackers for a thicker soup
  7. Salt to taste
  8. Serve with freshly cooked rice
