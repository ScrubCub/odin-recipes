Adobo

Description

This popular Filipino dish boasts a simple and easy-to-follow recipe but with complex flavours. Definitely a good dish to start when starting out with Filipino cuisine.

Ingredients

  • 250 g pork cutlets
  • a tablespoon of oil
  • garlic, diced
  • onion, diced
  • laurel leaves
  • peppercorn
  • (optional) hard-boiled eggs
  • one cup soy sauce
  • half cup vinegar
  • salt

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Add garlic and onion until slightly browned
  2. Add pork cutlets and let fry until slightly browned
  3. Add soy sauce and vinegar and half a cup of water. Cover and let simmer for 15 minutes
  4. Add laurel leaves and peppercorn. Let simmer again for 5 minutes or until pork is tender
  5. Salt to taste and add hard-boiled eggs if desired. Serve with freshly cooked rice
